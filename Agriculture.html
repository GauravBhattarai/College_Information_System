Career in Agriculture Field in Nepal

The majority of the population rely on agriculture in Nepal, there is huge potential for agriculture specialist in Nepal. Agriculture is one of the most important assets of the economy. The country with an efficient agriculture system has leaped forward in economic development. A large number of skilled agriculture specialists manpower are needed in Nepal. Even undergraduate students can work as an agricultural officer, agro expert, field officer, laboratory specialist and so on. Whereas, graduate students can work in the different government organization as well as NGO/INGO's. There are different academic research centers where graduates can pursue their careers as an agriculture specialist.

With that being said, a developing country like Nepal has lots of scope in the agriculture field. If the government put the right manpower in the right job, then Nepal can move forward in the field of agriculture. Nepal has plenty of fertile land where different crops can be grown and export. The importance of agriculture study in Nepal is growing day by day and thereby is increasing the job prospects in the field of agriculture in Nepal.

Best Agriculture Colleges in Nepal

1. Lamjung Campus

Sundarbazar, Lamjung, Nepal
☏ 977-66-529313

Lamjung Campus is one of the best agriculture colleges in Nepal affiliated to Tribhuvan University (TU) and located at Sundarbazar, Lamjung, Nepal. It is also the branch of the Institute of Agriculture and Animal Sciences (IAAS). The college represents another ecological region of Nepal where pupils come to widen their intellectual horizon in agriculture typical of mid hill region of Nepal. The Campus is fortified with physical and trained human resources to provide standard agricultural education in this region.

The whole academic environment of the campus suits the needs of the students as well as researchers in the hill agriculture production system. Students are attracted to every part of the country to pursue their higher studies in the field of agriculture.

Offered Programs:
* Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) in Agriculture

3. Himalayan College of Agricultural Sciences & Technology (HICAST)

Balkhu Khola, near Khasi Bazaar, Kalanki - Kathmandu
☏ 01- 4273341

Himalayan College of Agricultural Sciences & Technology (HICAST) is also one of the best agriculture colleges in Nepal. It was established in 2000 to respond to the need for specialized human resources in agricultural and veterinary sciences. The college actively contributes to the attainment of sustainable livelihoods and development of the nation by producing theoretical and practical education and engaging in research that creates a positive impact on the industry and the people of Nepal.

HICAST's uses interactive and practical learning approaches to give students a balanced education that will benefit them throughout their careers. Since its launch, the college has produced more than 500 graduates, and many have pursued further education at the masters and Ph.D. levels. Graduates can be found in government sectors as well as in various I/NGOs in Nepal and abroad.

The main objective of the college is to produce academically sound and practically competent scholars, professional agriculturist, veterinary doctors, technologists, and management leaders able to alter challenges into opportunities moving along the contemporary time and working in close association with the communities concerned.

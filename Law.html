Best Law Colleges in Nepal


Law course is a study of all aspects of law and the justice system. Students of law will involve in the various legal profession in the future. The program equipped with skills such as social appreciation, research, analytical and communication needed to meet the demand of any legal career. The program emphasizes on the in-depth knowledge of the law and its applications. As a law student, you will learn how to tackle some of the most problematic conflicts and issues in modern society and morality.

Law or legal study is related to almost every area of our daily life. Touching upon issues relating to society, business, economics, politics, the environment, human rights, international relations, and trade. Law is not just for lawyers or in the courtroom as it affects all aspects of society: from the protection of life and liberty to corporate or international relations, law graduates are capable in taking many important roles in various professions.

Law Course in Nepal

In Nepal, Law programs are conducted by Tribhuvan University (TU), Kathmandu University (KU), Nepal Open University, and Purbanchal University (PU). Law course is designed to develop students' skills in thought & argument and critical analysis. The program covers subjects, such as Criminology, Human Right and International Law, Family Law, Law of Taxation, Banking Law and Legal Methods among others. The coursework comprises of lecture, question-answer, research, case study, seminar, discussion, and workshop.

Some of the major objectives of this program are listed below:

i) To enhance students' legal thinking so that they will be able to interpret and apply the law correctly
ii) To equip students with a comprehensive understanding and essential knowledge in the multiple areas of law, from national law to international law, public & private law and substantive & procedural law.
iii) To enable students to research legal issues proficiently
iv) To encourage students to function within the ethics of their profession
v) To produce academically sound manpower in the areas of law
vi) To make students aware of right and responsibilities
vii) To expose students with Master critical thinking, strong reasoning and analytical skills

Career in Law Course in Nepal

After completing the course, the law students have many options. A Law graduate can be a legal advisor, advocate, solicitor, and teacher or can get employment opportunities in various courts of law, government services. They can also work to bring social reforms and teach the same in society. Law graduates can also combine their degree with other disciplines like business administration, company secretary, etc. A student can be-
* Litigators
* Corporate lawyers
* Judiciary and Civil Service
* Social Worker

Best law colleges in Nepal Inside Kathmandu valley

1. Nepal law campus

Kathmandu, Exhibition Road, Nepal

Nepal Law Campus (NLC) is the oldest college imparting legal education in Nepal since 1954. NLC is proud of providing highest and competent leadership for the judiciary and another mechanism within the country and abroad.

The basic objective of legal education is to impart knowledge to the students seeking the degree of law. To achieve the objective, NLC is running after graduation a three-year Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.), a five-year Bachelor of Arts Bachelor of Laws (B.A.LL.B.), a two-year Master of Laws (LL.M.) and a three-year Master of Laws (LL.M.) programs.

NLC has highly qualified and dedicated faculties including professors, readers, and lecturers. The Campus produces thousands of law graduates who are engaged in different governmental and non-governmental organizations inside the country and abroad. Its products have held the highest positions in legal and administrative sectors like the Attorney General of Nepal, Chief Justice of Nepal, Justices of Supreme Court, judges and judicial officers in Nepal, acclaimed academicians in various renowned universities around the world and so on.

Programs Offered:

* Bachelor of Art Bachelor of Law (B.A.LL.B)
* Bachelor of Law (LL.B)
* Master of Law (LLM)

2. National Law College

Pulchowk - Lalitpur, Nepal

National Law College (NLC) has been providing Law education to the students with the view of uplifting legal education scenario in Nepal. National Law College Runs Bachelor of Arts which has been combined with Law, i.e. BA LLB in the undergraduate level and the Masters of Law abbreviated as LLM in the post-graduate level under the affiliation of Tribhuvan University.

The College aims to serve and produce skilled and professional individuals in the field of Law in Nepal with its updated teaching methodologies and practical approach. College is running by experienced and well-trained faculty members and an interactive way of learning.

National College of Law promises a strong foundation of legal education to its students through its strong management and administration as well as a balanced team of teachers and other faculty members.

Programs Offered:

* Bachelor of Arts / Bachelor of Laws (BA/LLB) - TU
* Masters in Law (LLM) - TU
* Ten Plus Two / 10+2 - Law

4. Kathmandu University School of Law
